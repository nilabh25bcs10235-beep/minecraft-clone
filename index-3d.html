🌲 Welcome to 3D Minecraft + AI

A first-person voxel world powered by Three.js and OpenRouter Fusion AI.

Mouse
Move mouse to look around
Click game to lock cursor
WASD / Arrows
Move forward, left, back, right
Space
Jump
Left Click
Break the block you're looking at
Right Click
